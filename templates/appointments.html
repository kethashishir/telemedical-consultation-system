Purpose

This form connects Patients and Doctors through the Appointments table.

  • Foreign key: patient_id references Patients
  • Foreign key: doctor_id references Doctors
  • The selected date must match the doctor's available days
  • A doctor cannot have two appointments at the same date and time
  • Appointments with medical records are locked from editing and deletion
